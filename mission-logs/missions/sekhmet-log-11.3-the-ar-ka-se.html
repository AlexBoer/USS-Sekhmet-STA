Summary

Vinshari boarders hit the bridge. The team fought back but briefly lost control of the ship as the Vinshari deployed mechanical spiders to hack the bridge consoles. Tennan overloaded his own console to blast off mechanical spiders. Evi in sick bay was able to remotely send a specific frequency that knocked sveral Vinshari unconcious.

With Engineering silent and time running out, Conrak and Vrenta weighed ejecting the warp core or even scuttling the ship. They chose neither. They beamed into the upper tier of Engineering and opened a surprise attack on the Vinshari below. The boarders fought back, but their leader failed to finish the hack needed to eject the core, and the Sekhmet retook Engineering.

In the aftermath, Captain J’lara hailed from the Vinshari shuttle who had aided them in the starship conflict before. Her Ha’kiv had been stolen and she needed transport and aid. She beamed aboard with her science officer Thaccin. Thaccin was a tall, blue-skinned figure with a horned halo: A Tilikaal.
